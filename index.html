Particle.io Mass Flasher

Instructions

Create a .json file with device IDs and their associated keys. You can use an example.json. All devices must be of the same type! For example, all device IDs must be IDs of Particle.io Photons. You cannot mix Photons, P1s and Electrons.

In the Particle.io Build IDE, choose a target device that matches the device type. In the "code" tab next to your App's name, click on the cloud icon that allows you to "Compile and download a firmware binary".

Make sure all of your target devices are connected to the Particle.io cloud. Load your device .json file and your firmware binary. After you have selected a firmware binary, you may click "Flash All" to begin. Each device will be flashed in sequence.

Status icons:

  •   Waiting to flash
  •   Flashing
  •   Incorrect access token
  •   Unable to flash (device is offline?)
