All your devices, at once

Add all of your device IDs and access tokens of one device type (Core, Photon, Electron or P1) to our example devices.json. Supply a firmware binary matching the device type, and deploy the firmware with one click.

Particle.io Mass Flasher

Flash all of your Particle.io devices with one firmware, with one click.

Usage notes

All devices must be of one type. You cannot mix Photons, P1s and Electrons since they use different compiled firmwares. Make sure all of your target devices are connected to the Particle.io cloud before flashing. Devices will be flashed in sequence. Offline devices will cause a long timeout.

Status icons:

  •   Waiting to flash
  •   Flashing
  •   Incorrect access token
  •   Unable to flash (device is offline?)
